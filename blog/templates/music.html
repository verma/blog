Tough Mudder Mix I - Daft Punk Alive 2007

March 4, 2011

I rearranged some of the tracks from Daft Punk's Alive 2007 for Tough Mudder workouts we've been doing at Fit2Live. Its nowhere near pristine since my mixing skills are shady and I used garageband :). I think its going to fall short on time for 120-30 sets. But still, a lot of energy in this mix. I hope it helps you squeeze that extra energy out during your workouts.

The Number - Sound.C

May 4, 2010

I made this track when we went to the hospital once to get Anamika checked up during our pregnancy, it was going to take around 4 hours and I didn’t really have that much to do. Yes, this track is THAT old. Suvan is now almost a year old. This track hasn’t been mixed or leveled up, so go easy on your speakers and on the track. Hope you like it!

This is my most recent track.

Drugs of Choice - Sound.C

I wanted to create more of a euro-progressive track with some psychedelic basslines. I think some of the listeners found the baseline to be a little too high. My monitors don’t really have good lowend, so I feel this track doesn’t sound as warm as I would like. I like it though, a lot of elements mixed together.

Hard Moon - Sound.C & Omni Vista

I was experimenting with vocals here. I got the acapella from ccmixter.org. I like it overall, this track also has a lot sounds which I’ve played myself. I am not much of a player so that’s kind of a big deal for me. Hope you like it.

East River - Sound.C & Kirsten Hersh

This track is by far my favorite. I think I just got lucky with this one. A lot of people liked it too. I think my favorite part about this track is the bassline along with the fake 303 affect which kind of accentuates the bassline at places in a more “grinding” kind of a way. I also love the way snares and hihats come into the track from just plain kick and bass in one of the parts.
